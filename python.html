Unit 1

Labrunner

Description: Use the arrow keys to move around. This lab was meant to be a template for other game runners.

Unit 2

LineRunner

Description: Use the up arrow to jump, the down arrow to move down, and the left and right arrows to move left and right. Each bomb decreases your score while each coin increases your score. If you hit a wall, you lose. You can't win, but you can get a high score by surviving as long as you can.

Unit 3

GroundRunner

Description: Use the arrow keys to move the guy around. Collect coins to earn score. Each coin increases your score by one. Reach a score of 5 before the timer runs out to win the game. Otherwise, it's game over.

Unit 4

DodgeRunner

Description: Use the arrow keys to move. Dodge the aliens and reach the bottom of the screen to win. If an alien touches you it's game over.
